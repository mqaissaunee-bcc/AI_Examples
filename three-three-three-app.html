The 3-3-3 method is a simple yet powerful daily productivity framework that helps you focus on what matters most while maintaining balance.

üìã How It Works

Each day, you commit to:

  • 3 hours on your most important project
  • 3 shorter tasks (15-30 minutes each)
  • 3 maintenance tasks (quick admin/upkeep items)

üéØ The Main Project (3 Hours)

This is your deep work block. Choose your most impactful project‚Äîsomething that moves the needle on your goals. This could be:

  • Writing a report or proposal
  • Learning a new skill
  • Working on a creative project
  • Developing a business strategy

üí° Pro Tip

Use the built-in timer to track your 3-hour block. Take short breaks every 45-60 minutes to maintain focus.

‚ö° Shorter Tasks (3 Tasks)

These are important but less time-consuming activities that need attention:

  • Returning important calls or emails
  • Quick meetings or check-ins
  • Planning and organizing
  • Brief research or reading

üîß Maintenance Tasks (3 Tasks)

Small but necessary items that keep your life running smoothly:

  • Updating your calendar
  • Filing documents
  • Quick household tasks
  • Responding to routine messages

‚ú® Why It Works

  • Prevents overwhelm: Limits your daily commitments to manageable chunks
  • Ensures progress: Guarantees time for your most important work
  • Maintains balance: Addresses different types of work and life needs
  • Builds momentum: Completing 9 items daily creates a sense of achievement

üèÜ Success Tips

Plan ahead: Set up your 3-3-3 list the night before or first thing in the morning.

Review daily: Reflect on what worked and what didn't to improve your system.

üöÄ Getting Started
