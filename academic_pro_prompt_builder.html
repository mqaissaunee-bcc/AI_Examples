📖 How to get the best results

Use this prompt as a starting point, not a one-shot answer. The best workflow:

1
Build your prompt here Be specific in the "Task Details" field — the more context you provide, the better AI output you will get.
2
Choose your AI platform Select from the dropdown above, then click Open in LLM. The prompt copies to your clipboard automatically.
3
Iterate and refine Ask the AI to revise, adjust tone, shorten, expand, or try a different approach. Treat it like a draft from a capable but imperfect colleague.
4
You are always the expert AI cannot verify facts, know your institution's policies, or carry your professional judgment. Always review and take ownership of final output.
