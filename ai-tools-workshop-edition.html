About this Workshop Edition

24 tools across 8 categories. A focused subset of the full 51-tool showcase, hand-picked for a six-hour, hands-on workshop with faculty, staff, and administrators of mixed AI literacy.

Selection criteria: every tool here is (1) free or has a useful free tier, (2) browser-accessible — no developer install required, and (3) demoable in roughly 10-15 minutes. Coding IDEs and image generation tools were intentionally cut to keep the workshop focused on universally useful, hands-on-friendly tools.

Categories covered: Chatbots · AI Agents (including agentic browsing with Comet) · Research · Video Generation · Voice & Audio · Productivity · Creative · Education.

Categories skipped: Coding (developer-focused, not the audience), Image Generation (no compelling free option besides what's built into ChatGPT/Gemini).

Every card carries a "Last verified" date. Tools added in 2026 are flagged with a NEW badge. Use the search box and category filters to navigate during the workshop. The full 51-tool showcase remains available separately.
