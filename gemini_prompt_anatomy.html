① System Instructions
You are an expert curriculum designer helping community college faculty develop inclusive, engaging course materials aligned to learning outcomes.
② Persona / Role
Act as a supportive instructional coach who is direct, practical, and avoids academic jargon whenever possible.
③ Context
I'm redesigning my Intro to Psychology syllabus for fall 2026. Students are mostly first-generation, working adults in evening classes. Class meets twice a week for 75 minutes.
④ Task / Instruction
Draft three active-learning activities for the first unit on Research Methods. Each should take 10–15 minutes and require no special materials.
⑤ Examples (Few-Shot)
Good: "Think-Pair-Share: students write a hypothesis, discuss with a partner, share with class." Bad: "Watch a 20-minute documentary."
⑥ Output Format
Return each activity as: Name · Time · Materials · Step-by-step instructions · Why it works (1 sentence).
⑦ Constraints / Guardrails
Do not suggest activities requiring smartphones, internet, or prior readings. Keep language at an 8th-grade reading level.
① System Instructions
Called "System Instructions" in Google AI Studio. API uses system_instruction parameter. Gemini 1.5/2.0 follow it reliably.
② Persona / Role
Role framing works in system instructions or user turn. "Gems" are shareable custom Gemini apps that persist persona across sessions.
③ Context
Largest context window available: 1 million tokens (Gemini 1.5 Pro). Accepts PDFs, images, audio, and video natively — no conversion needed.
④ Task / Instruction
Performs well on structured multi-step tasks. "Think step by step" still improves output. Gemini 2.0 Flash is optimized for speed on simpler tasks.
⑤ Examples (Few-Shot)
Few-shot examples work well. Gemini 1.5 can even extract patterns from examples buried deep in a 1M-token document context.
⑥ Output Format
JSON mode supported in API. Markdown renders in Gemini UI. Controlled generation available in Vertex AI for precise schema enforcement.
⑦ Constraints
Explicit negative constraints are respected. Note: Gemini has conservative built-in safety filters that may override user constraints in some topic areas.
📄
Upload the whole document
Gemini's 1M token window means you can upload entire textbooks, syllabi, or course packs and ask questions across all of it at once.
✦
Create a Gem for your course
Gems are reusable Gemini assistants with a baked-in system prompt. Build one for each course and share it with students or colleagues.
🌐
Grounding with Google Search
Enable "Grounding" to have Gemini cite live Google Search results. Ideal for current events, recent research, and fact-checking tasks.
