What is Claude?

Claude is Anthropic's AI assistant, built with Constitutional AI to be helpful, harmless, and honest. Named after Claude Shannon, the father of information theory, Claude is designed specifically for educational environments with advanced safety features and ethical guidelines.

Hands-On Activities

Activity 1: Learning Mode Tutorial (20 minutes)

Your Task: Experience Claude's educational approach firsthand.

Step-by-Step:

  1. Go to claude.ai and create a new Project
  2. Enable Learning Mode in your Project settings
  3. Try this educational prompt:
"I'm struggling to understand how to teach the concept of photosynthesis to my biology students. Rather than just giving me a lesson plan, can you guide me through thinking about the best approaches using Learning Mode?"
  1. Notice how Claude asks questions back instead of providing direct answers
  2. Engage with the Socratic questioning process
  3. Compare this to asking the same question without Learning Mode

Activity 2: Artifacts for Educational Content (25 minutes)

Your Task: Create interactive educational content using Claude's Artifacts feature.

Try These Prompts:

"Create an interactive HTML demonstration of the water cycle with animations and clickable elements that students can explore." "Build a simple JavaScript calculator that teaches order of operations by showing each step of complex calculations." "Design a timeline visualization of major historical events from 1945-2000 with hover details for each event."
  1. Watch how Claude creates live, editable content in the Artifacts panel
  2. Modify the generated content in real-time
  3. Save or export the artifacts for classroom use

Activity 3: Extended Thinking for Complex Problems (15 minutes)

Your Task: Use Claude's reasoning capabilities for advanced problem-solving.

Example Prompts:

"Use extended thinking to analyze the causes and effects of student retention challenges in community colleges, considering multiple perspectives and potential solutions." "Walk me through the mathematical reasoning for solving this calculus problem step-by-step: Find the maximum area of a rectangle inscribed in a semicircle of radius 5." "Help me think through the ethical implications of using AI in education, considering both benefits and potential concerns."

Watch Claude's thinking process unfold with detailed reasoning chains.

Activity 4: Document Analysis and Feedback (20 minutes)

Your Task: Use Claude's large context window for educational document processing.

Steps:

  1. Upload a student paper, curriculum document, or research article
  2. Ask Claude to analyze the document structure and content
  3. Request specific feedback or improvements
  4. Generate discussion questions based on the content
"Analyze this student essay and provide constructive feedback focusing on thesis development, evidence usage, and argument structure. Use Learning Mode to guide the student toward improvements."

Safety & Constitutional AI

🛡️ What is Constitutional AI?

Constitutional AI is Anthropic's approach to building safe, helpful AI systems. Instead of relying solely on human feedback, Claude follows a "constitution" of ethical principles derived from sources like the UN Declaration of Human Rights.

📜 Core Constitutional Principles

Freedom and Equality

Supporting responses that promote freedom and equality for all users

Privacy and Respect

Ensuring responses respect user privacy and personal data

Non-Discrimination

Avoiding bias or discrimination in all responses

Honesty and Accuracy

Prioritizing truthful information and admitting uncertainty

🎓 Educational Safety Features

  • Academic Integrity: Learning Mode encourages original thinking over shortcuts
  • Transparent Reasoning: Claude explains its thinking process and limitations
  • Uncertainty Acknowledgment: Admits when it doesn't know something
  • Harm Prevention: Built-in safeguards against inappropriate content
  • Privacy Protection: No training on user data by default

🔐 Data Privacy and Compliance

Educational Data Protection

  • No Training on User Data: Student conversations aren't used to train models
  • Short-term Retention: Data retained for maximum 90 days for functionality
  • Enterprise Security: SOC 2 compliance and encryption standards
  • FERPA Consideration: Features designed with educational privacy in mind

⚖️ Ethical Considerations

Responsible AI Use in Education

  • Promote Critical Thinking: Use Learning Mode to develop reasoning skills
  • Maintain Academic Integrity: Establish clear policies for AI assistance
  • Ensure Equity: Consider access and digital divide issues
  • Foster Transparency: Teach students about AI capabilities and limitations
  • Support Human Learning: Use AI to enhance, not replace, educational interaction
